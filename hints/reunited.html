Hints: Reunited

Stuck on the clues:
Each of the left side clues is contained in a right side clue. For example, “Complete” resolves to DONE, which is contained in Country in Southeast Asia, which is INDONESIA. In general, the words don’t have common roots.
Stuck on extraction:
A common technique in puzzlehunts is “doing it again” after the first step of the puzzle. Here, we have a bunch of short words (MEOW, etc.), just like in the first step of the puzzle.
Have the final words but still stuck
The clues (and in particular, the numbers) in the left column are still useful. Try reordering them in order of the corresponding words in the right column.
