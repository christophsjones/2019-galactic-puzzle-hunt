Word Search II

“Remember the word search we had last year? That sure was fun!” said Nathan.

“Didn’t we have two word searches?” said Josh.

“Wait...I thought we had eight?”

“Anyway,’ said Josh, “this time I want you to guess the pair of words I'm thinking of.”

“Pairs of words? Oh no! The first time was hard enough!”

“You mean the first two times,” Josh corrected. “Oh, I forgot: there’s a twist, too — but don’t worry, we can start with a practice round.”

“Can you help me guess his words?” Nathan whispers to you. “I’ll give you half the loot!”
